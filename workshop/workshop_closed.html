Winter/Spring 2023 Bioinformatics Workshop Series

The UIC Research Informatics Core is presenting a series of 1-day hands-on bioinformatics workshops. Common bioinformatics techniques and methods will be reviewed, with an emphasis on practical skills, quality control, and data interpretation.

Registration is closed. Thank you for your interest, however the workshops are full. Keep any eye out for announcements of future workshops that we are planning to have.
